How it works

Tornado Cash v2 in three steps

Use Tornado Cash v2 as a direct app workflow: connect, compare, and sign only after the live route makes sense for your wallet.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect a self-custody wallet. You stay in control of approvals and signatures.

2

Choose the swap

Select ETH, BTC, or another supported asset path. The app builds the available route and shows live quote details before action.

Review and sign

Check destination, timing, cost, and chain context. Confirm from your wallet only when the transaction details match your intent.
